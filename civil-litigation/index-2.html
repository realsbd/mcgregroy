Civil litigation

You are here: Home » Civil litigation

While we always try to look for ways to resolve disputes without going to Court, we are committed to represent our clients proactively across a range of litigation issues.

We can provide the necessary representation if you do need to go to Court, and we can also assist in the negotiation and finalisation of settlements, in a range of different types of dispute. If you wish to settle or come to an agreement outside of court, we can arrange mediation to help you achieve a suitable resolution outside of the courtroom.

How we can help?

  • Disputes about Wills and Estates – sometimes there are disagreements about a Will or how an Estate is administered and we can help to resolve problems.
  • Financial maintenance for a dependant when someone dies – we can help advise you if you are an Executor who needs to respond to a claim or a dependent who requires advice see Wills & probate.
  • Landlords – if you have problems with either residential or commercial tenancies, we have expertise in resolving this type of dispute. From advice on tenant eviction and rent collection to lease extensions, our solicitors can provide tailored advice in a language that you can understand.
  • Property disputes – The land you own is usually your most valuable asset – so access to experienced, professional legal advice is essential if you are involved in a dispute about things such as boundaries, rights of way or trespass or nuisance by a neighbour.
