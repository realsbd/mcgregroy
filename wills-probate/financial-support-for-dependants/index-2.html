Financial support for a dependant when someone dies

It is often vital on the death of a family member that a dependant receive financial support. McGregroy lawyers are experienced in dealing with these cases to include providing representation in contested Court proceedings under the Inheritance (Provision for Family and Dependants) Act 1975.

McGregroy lawyers will provide sensitive, expert and cost effective advice and assistance in these cases.

Our Private Client team are highly experienced in dealing with these often difficult cases. To contact us, please telephone one of the numbers below or use the online enquiry form and we will contact you.

If you would prefer to see someone at home, or if you are in hospital or a care home, we can visit you at home at no extra cost. We will provide you in advance with information about the cost of our services.
